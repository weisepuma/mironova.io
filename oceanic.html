PROJECT

Oceanic

A whale tracking application that allows you to choose your favorite whale and track its movements.

About the Project

Oceanic is a whale tracking application that connects people with these magnificent creatures. Users can select their favorite whale and follow its journey across the oceans in real-time.

Features

  • Real-time whale tracking on interactive maps
  • Choose and follow your favorite whale
  • Learn about different whale species
  • Track migration patterns and behaviors
  • Community of whale enthusiasts
  • Donate to nature conservation and improve whale lives
